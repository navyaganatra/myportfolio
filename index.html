about me

I'm a Graphic Design student at ISDI, passionate about creating visual stories that balance concept and craft. My work explores the intersection of typography, layout, and storytelling — where design becomes both communication and emotion.

I love experimenting with bold visuals, thoughtful details, and design systems that feel human yet refined.

Ceylune Tea

Packaging Design

This was a packaging I made for an imaginary tea company based in Sri Lanka. It was inspired by a Rococo color palette and therefore uses pastel blues and pinks heavily.

The font and illustrations are apt to a sweet blended balance between Sri Lankan culture and Rococo aesthetics.

Diwali Edition

Packaging & Branding

This packaging was made for JD's Old No.7 for the Diwali season. It resembles the back of a playing card, as playing cards are a tradition for Diwali parties.

I used black, gold, and white to stay consistent with the brand while adding festive luxury.
